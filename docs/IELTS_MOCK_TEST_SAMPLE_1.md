# IELTS Mock Test Sample 1

## Təsvir

Bu, IELTS imtahanının tam strukturuna uyğun hazırlanmış sadə bir mock test nümunəsidir.

## Struktur

### 1. Listening Section (30 dəqiqə)
- **4 Part**, hər biri 10 sual = **40 sual cəmi**
- Part 1: Conversation (Q1-10) - Everyday social context
- Part 2: Monologue (Q11-20) - Everyday social context  
- Part 3: Conversation (Q21-30) - Educational/training context
- Part 4: Academic Monologue (Q31-40)

### 2. Reading Section (60 dəqiqə)
- **3 Passage**, **40 sual cəmi**
- Passage 1: Questions 1-13 (Easier text)
- Passage 2: Questions 14-26 (Medium difficulty)
- Passage 3: Questions 27-40 (Harder academic text)

### 3. Writing Section (60 dəqiqə)
- **2 Task**
- Task 1: Describe graph/chart (minimum 150 words)
- Task 2: Essay (minimum 250 words)

### 4. Speaking Section (11-14 dəqiqə)
- **3 Part**
- Part 1: Introduction and interview (4-5 minutes)
- Part 2: Long turn with cue card (3-4 minutes)
- Part 3: Discussion (4-5 minutes)
